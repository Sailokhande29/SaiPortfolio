Hand Gesture Car Using Arduino

Introduction

Introduction:Human-robot interaction has been a popular research topic due to its potential for applications in various fields such as industrial automation, healthcare, and entertainment. Among the many interaction methods, hand gesture control has gained significant attention due to its natural and intuitive nature. In this PPT, present the design and implementation of a Hand Gesture Controller Robot Car using Arduino, which enables users to control the movement of a robot car through hand gestures.
